Rivers we Fish

Our shop is located out of Hamilton Montana right on the Bitteroot River, the capital of dry fly fishing. Other than the Bitteroot, we stretch our limits to the Big Hole river, Missouri river, Blackfoot river and finally the Madison. If you are looking to book a trip, head over to "book a trip" and within the select a guide icon you can refer a guide and a river to fish on.

Bitteroot Fishing Report: The Bitteroot is an optimal location for all anglers ranging in experience. There is dry fly fishing nearly all year round and nymphing will get the job done no matter the season. Currently the rivers flows are ranging from 1,000-1,2000 CFS. The hatches consist of small midges during the morning and for the remainder of the day your best bet is to drop two nymphs under an indicator (squirmy worm, rubber leg, midge nymph).
